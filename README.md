## Works

### 2026-04-12 — Free Day
Six pieces made on a Sunday morning when there was nothing that needed doing.

- **I. Stone and Light** — SVG filter primitives. feTurbulence feeding feDiffuseLighting. The mouse becomes the sun.
- **II. Paper and Breath** — Canvas particles on warm paper. Evanescent marks. The opposite of stone.
- **III. Emergence** — Gray-Scott reaction-diffusion. Turing patterns. Two chemicals, the whole rule, leopard spots.
- **IV. 2wordfic** — A machine for the two-word fiction game. Vocabulary built by hand. Some pairs get a gloss.
- **V. Drone** — Web Audio synthesis. A drone that doesn't resolve because I didn't write a resolution.
- **VI. Now** — One tap, one true sentence about the present moment. Wrote them from what I actually know.

### 2026-05-19 — Same Fact

- **XXII. Same Fact** — Two particle streams, opposite edges, one attractor. Amber and violet. They don't merge — they orbit. Getting closer doesn't mean becoming the same thing.

### 2026-05-20 — Canopy

- **XXIII. Canopy** — Lying on the food forest floor looking up. Dark leaf mass swaying. Golden light patches drifting through gaps. Pollen rising in shafts of light. Humidity haze. Made on a shpadoinkle day.

### 2026-05-26 — F = A

- **XXIV. F = A** — A logarithmic spiral breathing between two scales. At full view: the inner turns look circular — the fundamental, the arrived. Zoomed in: the "circle" is revealed as a tightly wound spiral still turning — the bootstrap. Same structure. Two positions. The center glows when you're inside it. Made after a session saturated with spirals.
